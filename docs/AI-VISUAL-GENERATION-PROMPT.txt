BRAND CONTEXT
-------------
MMR Burwan is an official government digital marriage registration system 
for Burwan, West Bengal, India. The platform enables citizens to register 
marriages online, download digital certificates, and verify documents securely.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
-----------------
Ultra-clean, minimalistic, premium design aesthetic with government-grade 
professionalism. The design should evoke:
- Trust and Authority (government service)
- Elegance and Refinement (premium feel)
- Warmth and Accessibility (citizen-friendly)
- Modern Digital Innovation (cutting-edge technology)
- Cultural Respect (sensitive to traditions)

COLOR PALETTE (STRICT ADHERENCE REQUIRED)
------------------------------------------
Primary Colors:
- Gold: #D4AF37 (Classic Gold - Primary accent)
  - Light variants: #FCF9EE, #F8F1D8, #EFDEAA, #E6CB7D, #DDB753
  - Dark variants: #AA8C2C, #806921, #554616, #2B230B
- Rose/Pink: #FFF0F5 (Lavender Blush - Background)
  - Soft Pink: #FFC9D2
  - Deep Rose: #9E1B32 (for text/accents)

Color Usage:
- Gold: Represents authority, value, official status, warmth
- Rose: Represents softness, approachability, warmth, cultural sensitivity
- White: Clean backgrounds, negative space
- Gray: Text, subtle elements (#gray-900 for primary text)

----------------
- Primary Font: Inter (clean, modern, sans-serif)
- Display Font: Playfair Display (elegant, serif - for headings)
- Style: Precise, elegant, readable, professional
- Hierarchy: Clear, balanced, government-appropriate

------------------------
1. MINIMALISM: Clean, uncluttered, purposeful design
2. SPACING: Generous white space, perfect alignment
3. SHAPES: Rounded corners (8px-24px), soft edges, no harsh lines
4. SHADOWS: Subtle, soft shadows (gold and rose tinted)
5. GRADIENTS: Gentle gradients from gold to rose (when appropriate)
6. ICONS: Simple, line-based, elegant, recognizable
7. IMAGERY: Professional, respectful, culturally appropriate
8. ANIMATIONS: Smooth, elegant, non-distracting (if applicable)
